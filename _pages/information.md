## Location

MapNet will be held in the in the Te Toki a Rata (https://goo.gl/maps/c8pcsTwHtF8RyR5u9) Building lecture theatre (TTR L1) on the Kelburn Campus at Victoria University of Wellington.

## Travel

Wellington airport (WLG) is about 9 km to Victoria. Taxis from the airport cost $50+ and take half an hour or so. There are also shared shuttle services which can drop you in the centre of town or at your accommodation for about $18â€“25, and take around an hour.
